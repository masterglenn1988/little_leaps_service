Service Details

We provide personalized and structured support through our Individualized Development Program, focusing on skill development, positive behavior, early learning, and academic growth to help each child progress with confidence at their own pace.

Basic Skill Development Behavioral Tutoring Playschool Academic Tutorial

Basic Skill Development

Our Basic Skill Development Program is designed to strengthen foundational skills through our Individualized Development Program, tailored to meet each child’s unique needs and abilities. We focus on improving language, cognitive skills, motor development, social behavior, and daily living skills to help children grow at their own pace.

Behavioral Tutoring

Our Behavioral Tutoring Program is part of our Individualized Development Program, designed to support children in building self-control, attention, and appropriate social behaviors. We use structured strategies and positive guidance to help reduce challenging behaviors and promote healthy emotional development.

Playschool

Our Playschool / Small Group Learning Program is designed to support early learning in a structured yet fun setting. Through our Individualized Development Program, each child receives guided instruction while learning to cooperate, share, follow routines, and participate in group activities.

Academic Tutorial

Our Academic Tutorial Program is part of our Individualized Development Program, focusing on core subjects such as reading, writing, and mathematics. We provide structured guidance and one-on-one or small group instruction to help students improve understanding, focus, and academic performance.
