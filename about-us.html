About Us

At Little Leaps Learning Center, we support children and families through compassionate, evidence-based behavioral modification. Our team provides individualized development programs designed to help children build communication, social, and everyday skills in a positive and supportive environment. We believe in celebrating progress at every step and partnering with families to help each child reach their full potential one little leap at a time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to walk alongside children with special needs through personalized one-on-one support, nurturing their strengths, guiding them through challenges, and helping them build meaningful skills for school and everyday life.

Our Vision

We dream of school communities where children with diverse learning needs are understood with compassion, supported with intention, and empowered to grow confidently at their own pace
